What Makes Us Different

Who are we?

We are a group students pursuing undergraduate courses from premium institutes of India. Having done our preparations from Kerala itself, we came together to solve the problems faced by students preparing for different examinations through our own personal experiences. Our goal is to improve the size of the Malayalee community in premiere institutes in the country.

Our Lecture series

Designed and delivered by us, we have created this series keeping in mind the position of the student on their journey. With keen emphasis on foundational concepts, we will ensure that each and every student is satisfied through the experience provided. The lectures will also include key tips and tricks required to solves the questions in the paper.

Our helping hand

The resource materials are created by accumulating key concepts and questions from standard reference materials. Our materials have been designed to ensure that it will also fill the gaps in concepts required for your board exams

Mentoring:On the right path

We have created a detailed orientation programme for the students to help them reach the best of colleges to continue with their dream education. The programme will also provide crucial information about other valuable courses offered by other colleges than the one through JEE and NEET. This is done based on the experiences we have collected as students which could have been extremely helpful during the time of our preparations.
