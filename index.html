How we work

What Makes Us Different

Who are we??

We are a community of students pursuing Undergraduate degree in India's premium Institutes. Our goal is to revolutionise the current education system of Kerala and address the problems faced by students of our community across the state. For years we have observed that the quantity of students enrolling in these institutions per year from Kerala is far less.We are on a mission to improve this situation by providing right guidance and mentoring to each and every student .

Our Lecture series

We have designed a series of lecture that would lay the foundation stone for your preparation and help in grasping the basic concepts.The lectures are prepared and taken by IITians and MBBS students who have already crossed the hurdle and reached their dream institutions in their own style.

Our helping hand

Our resource materials are prepared by hand using reference materials of good standard and value which will help you in knowing and understanding different concepts. This will help you not just for your entrance preparations but also give you the additional knowledge that you may lack for your boards.

Mentoring:On the right path

You all may be aware about IIT-JEE and NEET-UG, but are these the only entrance exams that we can attend? Our orientation program will guide you through the various entrance exams that are conducted across our country, how to apply for these exams and most importantly, how to prepare yourself to attain the results you dream off.
